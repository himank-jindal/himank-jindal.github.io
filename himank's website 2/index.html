It is the number one tennis match in India.
Many people from many states come yo play in a particular state and the match goes for 5-6 days because there are about 20 players.

Goldslam is the second best tennis association match played in India.

Types of international matches

Australian Open

Australian Open is the first grandslam held in January.
It is held in Melbourne, Australia.
The tournament is the first of the four Grand Slam tennis events held each year excepting 1986.

French Open

French Open is also known as Ronald Garros held in May.
It is held in Paris, France.
This grandslam is the current tournament held in a Clay Court.
It is the conclusion of the spring clay court season.

Wimbledon

Wimbledon is the third grandslam.
Since the Australian Open shifted to hardcourt in 1988, Wimbledon is the only major still played on grass.
t has been held in Wimbledon, London, since 1877.

U.S. Open

U.S. Open is the last tournament held in U.S.A is a hard court tournament.
The tournament is the modern version of one of the oldest tennis championships, for which men's singles and men's doubles were first played in 1881.
